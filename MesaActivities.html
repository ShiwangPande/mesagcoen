MESA: Mechanical Engineering Student Association

MESA is a student organization that aims to provide a platform for students to learn and grow in the field of Mechanical Engineering. We are a group of students who are passionate about Mechanical Engineering and are always looking for opportunities to learn and grow. Activities

MESA organizes various activities throughout the year to help students learn and grow in the field of Mechanical Engineering. These activities include:

  • Workshops
  • Guest Lectures
  • Industrial Visits
  • Technical Events
  • Technical Competitions
